The Issue

Definition: Consumerism is an ideology that emphasizes the consumption of material items as a means of advancing satisfaction

Environmental impact: consumer culture accompanies a tendency to dispose of goods and replace them with trendier ones. The disposed goods end up in the landfill, and expanding the landfill encroaches on animal habitats. Additionally, Minority and low-income areas often live near landfills, and emissions from the landfills pose health risks and can cause malformations in children
