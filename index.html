Sustainable Living

The aim of Sustainable Living is to empower individuals to break free from consumerism and overconsumption by reducing waste and consumption, as well as fostering a culture that values people and the planet over possessions. This website outlines the issue of this social and economic ideology, and how to take action to reduce environmental impact.
